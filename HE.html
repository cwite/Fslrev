Organic farming

For Farming to be called organic it must: Not use artificial chemical fertilisers
not use Genitically Modified crops or Ingredients
Restrict the use of pesticides
Respect animal welfare
Guarantee a free range lifestyle for farm animals
Raise a diversity of crops and animals
Not use drugs, antibiotics or wormers
