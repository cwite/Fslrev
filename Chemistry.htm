Changing State The Melting Point Of A substance is the temperature at wehich it melts
Temperature does not change during melting as heat energy is used to overcome the attractive forces between the particles
Melting (Solid -> Liquid) And Freezing> (Liquid -> Solid)
The Boiling point of a substance is the temperature at which it boils
Temperature does not change during boiling as heat energy is used to overcome the attractive bonds between the particles
Boiling (Liquid -> Gas) And Condescending> (Gas -> Liquid) occur at the same temperature
Evaporation> (Liquid -> Gas) occurs at temperatures below the boiling point of the Liquid eg: puddle on cold day
